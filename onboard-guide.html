Onboarding Prep

Let’s get you ready to launch

A short checklist of what to gather and a couple of accounts to set up. Have these ready and your first week of content can go live fast.

What to have ready

You don’t need to format any of this — just have it on hand. We’ll capture it together during onboarding.
Your business
  • Business name and the city or area you’d like mentioned in posts
  • Website and your industry (so captions sound right for your field)
  • Booking or scheduling link, if you take appointments or sell tickets (optional)
Where you post
  • Your Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn — whichever you use
Your voice
  • A sentence or two on how you’d describe your business and the tone you want (warm, expert, playful…)
  • Who your customers are, and what you offer (services, classes, menu items)
  • Anything we should never say — words, claims, or topics to avoid
  • Any current promotion or offer you’d like featured (optional)
Visuals
  • A set of your best photos — we’ll walk you through uploading them, no prep needed beyond gathering them
  • Brand colors or a banner image for your emails (optional — we can pick from a palette)

Connect Buffer

We schedule and publish your posts through Buffer, so we’ll need access to your channels.
1
Create a Buffer account. If you don’t already use it, sign up for free at buffer.com.
2
Add your channels. Inside Buffer, connect your Facebook Page, Instagram, and LinkedIn. Instagram must be a Business or Creator account to schedule — it’s free to switch in the Instagram app under Settings → Account type.
3
Create an API access token. In Buffer’s settings, generate an access token for us. When it asks how long the token should last, choose the longest option — 1 year.
Pick the 1-year expiration for the Buffer token. A shorter one quietly expires and publishing stops without warning — the longest option keeps your content flowing.

Your email platform

We send your newsletters and promo emails through your own email marketing platform.

We plug straight into Kit and Mailchimp. If you use either one, just have your API key ready to share during onboarding.

Use a different email platform — Constant Contact, Brevo, ActiveCampaign, Flodesk, or something else? Let us know before onboarding so we can build the integration for it in time.

That’s it

Gather the items above and set up Buffer, and we’ll handle the rest together on your onboarding. Questions before then? Just reply to the email that sent you here.
