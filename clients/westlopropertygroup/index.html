How Private Lending Works

A typical investment moves through four clear milestones, start to finish.

1

Deal Sourcing

We identify highly undervalued residential and multifamily assets, 30–50% below market value.

2

Escrow & Legal

Your capital is placed in escrow. All lien documents record prior to close, securing your position.

3

Execution

Our acquisition, construction, and sales teams handle the renovation, management, and disposition.

4

Maturity

Your principal is returned along with fixed interest gains at closing of resale or refinance.

Your Margin of Safety

How Your Capital Is Protected

Every dollar you place is secured by a stack of legal protections recorded before close.

✓

Promissory Note

A personal guarantee and legal contract defining the debt repayment terms.

✓

Deed of Trust / Mortgage

Legally recorded with the county, providing a 1st or 2nd collateral position against a hard physical asset.

✓

Hazard Insurance Policy

Names you as Loss Payee, insulating your capital from natural disasters.

✓

Title Insurance

A full title policy ensures clean ownership transfer and zero junior-lien vulnerability.

Informed Investing

Frequently Asked Questions

What interest rates do you pay lenders?

Rates are typically between 8–15%, depending on the project.

How long will my funds be held?

Loans generally run on a 6–12 month note, depending on project size, with options to extend.

Is there a guarantee on my investment?

We personally guarantee each loan, in addition to the recorded collateral position.

Can I use a retirement account?

Yes. A self-directed IRA can invest in real estate; we recommend a qualified custodian for tax-deferred investments.

Will my money be pooled with other investors?

No. Funding is tied to one property, secured by its own deed of trust.

What does it cost me to lend?

The company covers all closing costs. There are no fees or commissions for private lenders.
