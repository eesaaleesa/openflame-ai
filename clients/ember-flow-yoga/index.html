Our Studio

What we're about

Ember Flow was founded on a simple idea: yoga should feel like coming home, not auditioning. We're not a brand. We're a small studio on Alberta Street with good floors, warm light, and teachers who learn your name.

The room holds about twenty people on a full night. You'll see the same faces across a season. You'll find a teacher who fits your body. You'll figure out that yoga is mostly about paying attention to what's already happening.

Beginners belong in every class on our schedule — no separate room, no separate time. Just here, with everyone else, figuring it out together.
