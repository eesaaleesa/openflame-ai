Process

Simple for you.
Sophisticated underneath.

A 5-agent AI system runs continuously in the background. Your job is a 30–60 minute daily review.

01

Research

Our AI agents continuously monitor Portland fitness trends, local events, seasonal hooks, and what's resonating with your audience. No stale, generic content — everything is grounded in what's actually happening in your city.

02

Create

Content is written in your specific voice — your tone, your terminology, your values. We onboard deeply at the start and refine over time. The result reads like you wrote it on your best day, every day.

03

Review & Publish

You review each day's content in 30–60 minutes, request any tweaks, and approve. We handle scheduling and publishing. You stay in control without being in the weeds.

No black box. You see everything before it goes out. The AI does the heavy lifting; you maintain final say on your brand. That's how it should work.
